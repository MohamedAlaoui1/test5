About Mohamed

ŸÅŸÜÿßŸÜ Artist

Born in Marrakech, raised on the hypnotic rhythms of Gnawa music and the pulsating beats of electronic dance music.

Mohamed Alaoui represents a new generation of artists bridging the gap between traditional Moroccan sounds and contemporary electronic music. His unique style fuses the spiritual trance of Gnawa ceremonies, the intricate patterns of Andalusian melodies, and the energy of modern house and techno.

From the ancient medinas of Fes to the world's biggest festivals, Mohamed carries the soul of Morocco wherever he performs, creating immersive sonic journeys that transport audiences across time and space.
